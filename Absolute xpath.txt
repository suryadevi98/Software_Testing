
 Absolute X-path
 ===============

  * It starts with single forward slash (/)

  * It starts from html tag (Root Tag)

 Example
 =======
      
        /html/body/div[2]/div[1]/div/h4[1]/b[1]

   <html> -> open   , </html> -> close


  Example:
  =======

 facebook
 ========

 email : /html/body/div[1]/div[1]/div[1]/div/div/div/div[2]/div/div[1]/form/div[1]/div[1]/input
 password : /html/body/div[1]/div[1]/div[1]/div/div/div/div[2]/div/div[1]/form/div[1]/div[2]/div/input

login : /html/body/div[1]/div[1]/div[1]/div/div/div/div[2]/div/div[1]/form/div[2]/button
